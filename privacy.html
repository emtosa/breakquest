Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Break Quest: Breathe & Loot ("the App") is designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

No Data Collection

Break Quest does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. The App has no network requests related to user data and requires no account or registration of any kind.

What Stays on Your Device

All information generated by your use of the App — including:

  • Work and break session history
  • Loot items earned through breathing breaks
  • Wizard room progression and cosmetic state
  • App preferences and settings

— is stored exclusively on your device using standard iOS local storage. This data never leaves your device.

No Third-Party SDKs

Break Quest contains no advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks, crash reporting services, or any other third-party code that could transmit data. The App is entirely self-contained.

No Accounts Required

There is no sign-up, login, email address, or any form of identity required to use Break Quest. You open the app and begin immediately.

Offline Operation

Break Quest works fully without an internet connection. There are no server-side dependencies for any core functionality.

Children's Privacy

Because Break Quest collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages. No personal information is gathered from any user, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this URL. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any revisions.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the App, please visit the App Store page: https://apps.apple.com/app/id6759697192
