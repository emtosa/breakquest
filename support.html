Support & FAQ

Answers to common questions about Break Quest: Breathe & Loot.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the work/break cycle work?

Work for 25 minutes with the timer running. When the session ends, a 5-minute break starts automatically. During the break, a guided breathing mini-game activates. Complete 2 breathing cycles to unlock your loot reward.

What is the breathing mini-game?

An animated breathing guide paces you through a calming in-hold-out cycle. Follow the visual cues to breathe in sync. It's designed to bring your nervous system down after a focus sprint — and it's actually pleasant.

What kind of loot can I earn?

Cosmetic treasures for your wizard's room: glowing orbs, tiny familiars, magical artifacts, enchanted furniture, and more. Each piece is unique and only earnable through completed breathing breaks.

What is the Treasure Room?

Your wizard's cozy room fills with every loot piece you earn. It's a visual record of every break you actually took. The more you rest, the richer your room becomes.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, completely. Break Quest works with no internet connection. All session data and your loot collection stay on your device.

Does it collect any data?

No. 100% local. No accounts, no analytics, no ads.
